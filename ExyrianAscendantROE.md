### General Rules and Conduct
* The instructions of our OICs and officers are to be followed at all times.
* Annoying asshole clause: Pereo is open to combat, not verbal abuse and harassment. Communication should be relayed only to the OIC, and it must be at least civil.
* We may ask you to stop using any item until it can be reviewed. Use may resume if all parties involved agree.
* Beta-test or pre-release equipment should be not be used without first obtaining the defending OIC's consent. We do not permit in-field script editing from attackers during an ongoing fight.
* Attackers are free to use any [EX] provided equipment, especially in cases where their own equipment is rejected for use.
* Teleporting or medicing is prohibited.
* Do not enter red spawn zones, and do not shoot into them unless you are returning fire.
* No loud or obnoxious sounds are allowed.
* Avatars must be at least 1.5 meters tall and represent age-appropriate characters.
* Prejump must be enabled.

### Banned Equipment
* Agent list guns are prohibited; sensor-based weapons must have line-of-sight checks.
* Weapons designed to kill through obstacles that would normally stop them are prohibited.
* Seeker-style munitions may not be used against infantry.
* Fully automatic raycast weapons are not allowed at this time.

### Movement Enhancers
* Jetpacks: **(Jetpacks are not permitted at Pereo at this time - this is a framework for future use)**
  - Cannot recharge while in the air.
  - Should only move the avatar a reasonable distance in any direction (the full width of a region is not considered reasonable).
* Jump packs/ Boost packs: **Permitted**
  - Keep your boostpack reasonable. It should be a BOOST pack, not a catapult-yourself-across-the-sim pack.
  - If your own boostpack goes outside what we consider "reasonable", or you don't have one, we provide a copy for free use in the attacker spawn. You're welcome to grab one.
* Grapple Hooks: **Permitted**
  - Ex's current grapple has a range of 32 meters and a cooldown of 4 seconds. It would be courteous to ensure that your grapples have similar constraints.
* Sprints:
  - Must be reasonable, and should not be abused to the detriment of fair combat.
* Diverolls & Dashes:
  - Maximum distance of 15 meters.
  - Cannot be performed in the air.
  - Minimum recharge time of 6 seconds.

### Weapons and Equipment
* All weapons must have some kind of coded cooldown or reload.
* Shotguns should fire prim bullets or use raycast.
* Bullets must not intentionally penetrate walls, barricades, or shields designed to stop them.
* Any type of temporary deterrent, such as smoke, flashbangs, flames, etc, should never last more than 10 seconds.
* Strict movement inhibitors on equipment or its effects are not permitted.
* Healing abilities are not currently permitted.
* Riot shields are allowed but must not exceed 50 HP, must take damage from standard collisions, and must be directional.

### Explosives and Area of Effect
* Explosives should have a kill radius of no more than 5 meters and a wound radius of no more than 10 meters unless highly directional or unless the equipment includes a wind-up time that is choreographed both visually and audibly for several seconds before launching/deploying.
* Explosives must not kill through walls or other solid objects.
* AoE and explosive damage must not be group-safe.
* Deployable explosives must have a maximum HP of 1 and be destroyable by standard projectiles.
* Deployable mines must be at least 0.25 x 0.25 x 0.04 meters in size.
* No flak weapons of any kind are permitted.
* Breach equipment is permitted.

### Deployables and Auto-Turrets
* Deployables must take damage from standard projectiles.
* Deployables may have a maximum HP of 100 at deployment. They may be repaired or constructed to higher values after deployment, up to a maximum of 300.
* Auto-turrets are not permitted.
* Mortars are permitted.

### Vehicles and Armor Systems
**(Vehicles are not permitted at Pereo at this time - this is a framework for future use)**
* All armored units (vehicles and avatars) must utilize LBA system; no others are permitted.
* Armored units may hover or use jetpacks no more than 15 meters above the ground.
* Self-repair or healing functions on armor layers on units must have a cooldown of at least 1 minute.
* Shields or shield layer regeneration should have a cooldown of 30 seconds.
* Tanks and heavy vehicles: maximum 300 HP.
* Fast attack and light vehicles: maximum 100 HP, must use LBA Light.
* Body armor that provides full 360° protection (Combat 2.0) is not currently permitted.
